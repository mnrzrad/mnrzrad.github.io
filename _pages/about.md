I am Mina Norouzirad, a dedicated researcher in Mathematical Statistics with a Ph.D. from Shahrood University of Technology, Iran, and currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at NovaMath, Universidade NOVA de Lisboa, Portugal. With expertise in innovative methodologies for statistical modeling, I focus on Shrinkage, Robust Regression, High-dimensional Analysis, and Machine Learning. My work has garnered recognition through peer-reviewed publications and awards, and I am passionate about mentoring the next generation of statisticians. Proficient in R, Python, Julia, and SPSS, I aim to contribute to the dynamic field of statistics, with an H-index (Google-Scholar) of 8 and over 180 citations, reflecting the impact of my research.
